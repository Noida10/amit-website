About Us

Empowering Hoteliers Worldwide

We're on a mission to make property management simple, efficient, and accessible for hotels of all sizes.

Our Story

Quickinn was born from a simple observation: hotel management software was either too expensive, too complicated, or both. Small hotels and independent properties were left behind, forced to use spreadsheets or outdated systems that couldn't keep up with modern guest expectations.

Founded in 2020, we set out to build a property management system that any hotelier could use - regardless of their technical expertise or budget. We believed that powerful technology shouldn't be reserved for big hotel chains alone.

Quickinn is designed to help properties of all sizes - from cozy B&Bs to boutique resorts - save time, increase revenue, and deliver exceptional guest experiences.

🎯

Our Mission

To democratize hotel technology by providing powerful, affordable, and easy-to-use property management solutions that help hoteliers of all sizes succeed in the modern hospitality landscape.

👁

Our Vision

To become the world's most trusted cloud PMS, empowering every hotel - from a 5-room guesthouse to a 500-room resort - to deliver exceptional guest experiences through innovative technology.
