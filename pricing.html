Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

Is there a free trial?

Yes! All plans come with a 14-day free trial. No credit card required. You can explore all features and upgrade anytime.

Can I change plans later?

Absolutely. You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate the difference.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers for annual plans.

Is there a setup fee?

No setup fees, ever. You only pay the monthly or annual subscription fee. We also provide free onboarding assistance.

What happens to my data if I cancel?

Your data remains accessible for 30 days after cancellation. You can export all your data at any time, and we'll help you with migration if needed.

Do you offer discounts for non-profits?

Yes, we offer special pricing for non-profit organizations and educational institutions. Contact our sales team for details.
